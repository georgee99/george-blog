Ranking my favourite flavours at Anita's Gelato after trying them all. This might offend some people, but oh well.

| Rank | Flavour | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 | Mango | It's the best fruit, and is way better than the artificial sorbet stuff. Enough said. |
| 2 | Salted Caramel | Incredibily rich. Has a sweet and salty balance. It's yum in moderation. |
| 3 | Hazelnut Mousse & Chocolate Crunch | Crunchy shards paired with hazelnut contrast very nicely. The white chocolate is also very sweet |
| 4 | Nougat Wafer | The hazelnut base and wafers are both high quality. The variation gives a multilayered impression. |
| 5 | Vanilla | Simply can't go wrong. Boring on paper, but it's Anita's most popular for a reason. |

### My takes
<div className="[&_li::marker]:text-neutral-900 dark:[&_li::marker]:text-neutral-100">
- Pistachio is crazily overrated. It's just a trend, I don't think anyone actually likes it
- Flavours with too much chocolate can make you sick
- Fruit flavours are hit or miss (mango hit)
- Whoever invented chocolate mint should go to jail
- Chunky toppings ruin the gelato
- Tiramisu is basic now
- Don't mix flavours and get upset when it tastes bad
</div>
